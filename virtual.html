To all students admitted to the Cornell Computer Science PhD Program,

On behalf of Cornell and the CS graduate student community, congratulations! Cornell is an excellent school, home to many enthusiastic researchers with diverse interests. We are looking forward to providing assistance as you consider your options for graduate studies.

We're sorry that you could not make it to our visit day, but we put together the most important points of the program for a virtual visit day. We also encourage you to reach out to faculty to set up meetings with them. You can see a list of all CS faculty on both campuses on the department faculty page.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at phdvisitday at list.cs.cornell.edu.

To receive an invite to our virtual visit day, please RSVP here: https://goo.gl/forms/Be9vzxVv2ZFZHpfs1.

See you soon!

Cheng, Claire, Dietrich, John, Jonathan, Julia, Shannon (Ithaca), Alane, and Eugene (New York)
(The Visit Day Czars)
