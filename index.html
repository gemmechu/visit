To all students admitted to the Cornell Computer Science PhD Program,

On behalf of Cornell and the CS graduate student community, congratulations! Cornell is an excellent school, home to many enthusiastic researchers with diverse interests. We are excited to host you as you consider Cornell for your graduate studies.

Our CS PhD visit days scheduled for March 27-29 are offered in a hybrid model, on both the Ithaca and the New York City campuses. Please review Cornell's visitor policy located here: https://covid.cornell.edu/ .

The below schedule is a work in progress.  Please continue to revisit this page for updates.  When appropriate we will provide zoom links to talks.  You will receive your personalized schedule prior to Monday, March 27 with office or zoom links to individual faculty meetings.

We are excited to meet you all either in person or virtually.  We hope you take a moment to visit our tabs above and watch the videos under "campus."

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at phdvisitday@cs.cornell.edu

See you soon!
